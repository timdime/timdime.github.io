Why Choose the Pistols?

🏆

Championship Legacy

One of the longest-running travel softball programs in the Chicagoland area with a proven track record of developing elite players and winning championships.

👩‍🏫

Coaching

Parent coaching staff dedicated to developing both individual skills and team chemistry. We focus on fundamentals, strategy, and sportsmanship.

⚾

Competitive Play

Face top-tier competition in tournaments and leagues throughout the region. Build confidence and skills against the best teams in the area.

Team Information

🎂 Age Group

12U Division - Girls born in 2013 calendar year

🏟️ Season

Spring through Fall with tournaments and league play

📍 Location

Park Ridge, Illinois and surrounding Chicagoland area

👥 Team Size

We are seeking 1 to 2 girls to join our core team, who is starting their 3rd year together. The girls are spread between Park Ridge and Chicago public and private school systems. A diverse group, where you will feel welcomed by the team and families.

🎯 Skill Level

Competitive travel ball experience preferred but not required for dedicated players with a few years experience in house league.

🏃‍♀️ Commitment

Regular practices, weekend tournaments, and league games.

What We're Looking For

We're seeking dedicated players who are passionate about softball and ready to take their game to the next level. Whether you're new to travel ball or have experience, we value hustle, coachability, and team spirit above all. Our program emphasizes skill development, competitive play, and building lifelong friendships through the love of the game.
