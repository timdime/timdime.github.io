Why Choose Chief Mobile Home Park?

Since 1950, the same family has owned and operated Chief Mobile Home Park, providing exceptional service and creating a safe, close-knit community for our residents.

Safe & Secure

Professional on-site staff and a secure environment ensure your peace of mind in our well-maintained community.

Prime Location

Close to major expressways, public transportation, schools, shopping, and just minutes from Midway Airport.

Affordable Living

Competitive pricing with low monthly land rental fees. Live comfortably for a fraction of traditional housing costs.

Community Feel

Join a friendly neighborhood where neighbors become friends. All ages welcome in our inclusive community.

Pet Friendly

Your furry family members are welcome! We understand pets are part of the family.

Full Service

City water and sewer, off-street parking, and professional maintenance services included.
